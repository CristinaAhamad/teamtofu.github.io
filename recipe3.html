Sugar Free Brownies

Description

A perfect sweet tooth-pleaser that lacks sugar for those who are diabetic. This recipe is so delicious you won't even miss the sugar!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup margarine
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup sugar free chocolate fudge flavored instant pudding
  • walnuts (optional)

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour an 8x8 inch pan.
  2. In a small saucepan over medium heat, melt margarine and cocoa together, stirring occasionally until smooth. Remove from heat and set aside to cool. In a large bowl, beat eggs until frothy. Stir in the instant chocolate pudding and vanilla. Combine the flour , baking powder, and salt; stir into the egg mixture then mix in the cocoa and margarine. Finally stir in the 1/4 cup of milk and if desired, the walnuts. Pour into the prepared pan.
  3. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes in the preheated oven, until a toothpick inserted into the center, comes out clean.
